# Advance Illinois Projects

This folder includes selected projects from my academic-year internship with Advance Illinois, where I worked on education policy research related to disparities in school discipline rates and non-public school enrollment.

## Projects Included

### CRDC Discipline Analysis

This project analyzes disciplinary actions for K-12 students across Illinois using Civil Rights Data Collection (CRDC) data. The analysis focuses on in-school suspensions, out-of-school suspensions, expulsions, and corporal punishment.

The project involved reviewing federal CRDC reporting guidelines, organizing discipline datasets, calculating trends, and analyzing disparities by race/ethnicity, gender, disability status, English Learner status, locale, and school type.

### Non-Public School Enrollment Validation

This project focused on validating an existing analysis of non-public school enrollment in Illinois. The initial analysis was based on Fall Enrollment Numbers, and I compared those figures with data from the U.S. Census and the Illinois State Board of Education (ISBE) Report Card.

I cleaned and cross-referenced datasets to identify discrepancies, confirm data accuracy, and strengthen the reliability of reported enrollment trends.
