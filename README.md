# Policy, Data, and Research Portfolio

This repository contains selected projects from my Master of Public Policy training at the University of Chicago Harris School of Public Policy and relevant internships.

## Repository Sections

### 1. Advance Illinois

Education policy projects from my academic-year internship with Advance Illinois, including CRDC discipline analysis and non-public school enrollment validation.

### 2. Coding and Data Projects

Coding and data analysis projects, including R Markdown work from the Urbanism Lab Old Chicago Post Office property value analysis.

### 3. GIS Portfolio

Spatial analysis portfolio with selected projects using QGIS, GeoDa, Python, ACS/Census data, and policy-oriented mapping.

### 4. Policy Memos

Selected policy memos and writing samples, including a Harris Core finalist group memo and a trauma-informed education policy memo.

### 5. Survey Research

Survey research materials, including a questionnaire and research proposal on refugee resettlement experiences and support adequacy.
