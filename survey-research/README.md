# Survey Research Design

This folder contains survey research materials focused on refugee resettlement experiences, public assistance use, and perceived adequacy of support among adult refugees resettled in the United States.

## Materials Included

- `Amjud_Survey Questionnaire.pdf`: Final self-administered questionnaire.
- `Amjud_Survey Research Proposal.pdf`: Research proposal describing the study motivation, research question, target population, constructs, and survey design approach.

## Project Summary

This project compares experiences of refugees resettled through private sponsorship pathways and traditional resettlement pathways. The survey focuses on economic dependency, use of public assistance, perceived adequacy of support, and transition experiences after arrival in the United States.

The questionnaire was designed as a self-administered paper survey, with attention to question wording, response options, visual layout, skip logic, respondent burden, and accessibility.

## Skills Demonstrated

- Survey questionnaire design
- Construct development
- Measurement of subjective and behavioral outcomes
- Refugee and migration policy research
- Response scale design
- Paper survey formatting
- Research proposal writing
- Ethical and accessible survey design

## Data Note

This folder contains survey design materials and does not include respondent data. The questionnaire is a course-based research design product and was not used to collect identifiable respondent information.
